    Smart Scale

    Smart scales have become a new affordable accessible way for people to get extensive body readings that would have been hard to get in the past. These smart scales work by sending small electrical impulses through the body, and depending on the time and amount, the scale can then determine your body composition. This consists of muscle mass, skeletal mass, water mass, and fat mass.

    Self Lacing Shoes

    Self-lacing shoes might seem like a gimmick, but it does serve as a tool for those who have disabilities that lack the muscle strength or motor functions to tie on their own shoes. Having a pair of shoes for someone dealing with ALS for example, would make their lives easier as they would not need as much help getting their shoes on.
